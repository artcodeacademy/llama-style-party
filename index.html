Party Llama-Style

For your first project we will create a party with music we can dance to. Click the link to see the example:

Click here

1) Create a new project, delete the cat object and choose your own object and backdrop. If you want you can choose the same ones as in the example.

2) When you press the green flag make the llama change to the next costume every half a second.

3) When you press the green flag make the llama glide to the right and then to the left half a second after.

Hint: The x represents the horizontal movement (left - right) and the y represents the vertical movement (up - down).

4) When you press the green flag make the backdrop change its color effect.

Hint: Look through the purple blocks (Looks).

5) Add a sound to make your party more fun, make sure that it plays until it finishes.

Hint: Look through the "Loops" section to find a longer song.

6) Finally, make the llama say something fun for 3 seconds like "Let's dance llama-style!"

Bonus: Try to personalize your party inviting more friends (more objects) and changing the music or backdrops.

*** Important: Always remember to save your project and share it when you're done.
